Yours Truly

Thanks for stopping by, great to see you! My name is Larry Evans and my journey began at the Georgia Institute of Technology, where my iniital studies culminated in the receipt of a Bachelors of Science degree in Management. Since that time, I have risen to the rank of AVP of Compliance Systems for one of the pioneering companies in nationwide online residential mortgage lending, where I have developed a vision for what sound web-based compliance and business solutions should look like. I've learned over that time that my interest didn't stop with merely envisioning a solution-- the real fun is building the solution! So over a decade later, I returned to where it all began, Georgia Tech, to channel my passion, unleash my creativity, hone my craft, and become a true application architect. It's going to be a fun ride!
